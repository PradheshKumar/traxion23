About Us

The Department of Automobile Engineering was formed in the year 1999 and offers a B.E degree in Automobile Engineering, with a mission to educate and impart practical knowledge to the students to become globally competent. The Department has well-qualified and experienced faculty and staff members of proven ability and administrative skills. The Department has state-of-the-art laboratories that cater to the needs of the students improving their research potential in the field of engine and vehicle testing. The Department is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
